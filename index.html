Physical oceanographer and incoming postdoctoral fellow at NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory, working under Jinbo Wang. Graduate school funding was supported by NASA through Future Investigators in NASA Earth and Space Science and Technology (FINESST).

My graduate advisers are the amazing Sarah Gille, Bruce Cornuelle, and Matt Mazloff. I could not imagine a better trio of scientists to guide me through my studies.
